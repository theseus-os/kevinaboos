Research Interests

I am a systems researcher interested in mobile operating systems, state management and synchronization, and virtualization. I'm currently researching a way to eliminate state entanglement, i.e., decoupling mobile applications from the underlying OS Service and other components on which they run. Synchronizing the states of applications and OS data structures in the mobile domain presents new challenges, namely low latency and energy efficiency requirements.

In the past I have worked on virtual reality, I/O virtualization, multiscreen streaming frameworks, static analysis of device drivers, software engineering tools for change impact analysis, and novel debugging procedures for mobile robotics.
